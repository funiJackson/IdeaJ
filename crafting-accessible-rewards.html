🎮 Phase 2A

Switched to Midjourney — More Descriptive Prompting

✦ Prompt 1 — Initial Concept

A clean, scalable vector pixel art icon of a small, friendly-looking trophy, rendered in a classic 8-bit or 16-bit gaming and anime mosaic style. Flat design, minimalist complexity. The trophy has a rounded, cheerful pixel star shape at its peak representing effort and rising skill. Color palette: soft blues, warm yellows, lavender, soft cyan. Strictly NO RED and NO GREEN. Flanked by two simple pixel wings suggesting upward progress. Designed for micro-size legibility (32×32pt). Solid white background. Absolutely no text.

✦ Prompt 2 — Refine

Keep the original style, remove the yellow stars and hearts, and leave the wings unchanged. Add a mosaic effect around the wings to indicate flapping flight; keep the flapping effect minimal.

Diffusion Iteration — Refinement

✦ Prompt 3 — Add Character

1. Remove the thickened lines at the top of the trophy to make lines uniform; keep everything else.
2. Add a cute yellow smiley face star to the top of the trophy — but only show half of its face, like a curious baby peeking out.

✦ Prompt 4 — Remove Wings

1. Remove the wings but retain the surrounding mosaic effect of wing vibration. Add a small amount of mosaic around the blank area where wings were removed — not too much.
2. Remove the yellow heart at the bottom.
3. Make the stars show a smiling face, but don't fully reveal the trophy.
